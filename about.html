Our Story

Your Safety and Comfort Are Our Mission

At Prime Ride Transit, we understand that missing a medical appointment isn't an option. Whether it's dialysis, physical therapy, or a routine check-up, getting there safely and on time can make all the difference.

We established Prime Ride Transit LLC after recognizing how often patients struggle with unreliable transportation. Delays, missed rides, and lack of professionalism are common issues in this space. We created this company to offer a higher standard of service built around consistency, accountability, and respectful treatment of every rider.

Today, we're proud to serve patients, families, and healthcare facilities across Florida with the same level of care we'd want for our own loved ones.
